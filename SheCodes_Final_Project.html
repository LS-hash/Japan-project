Japan

Learning Japanese language is difficult, but extremely rewarding

The country's name is made up of the characters 日 (pronunciation ni, "day, sun") and 本 (pronunciation hon, "origin, root, beginning"). Japan is therefore also known as the "Land of the Rising Sun" . The compound term can be pronounced both Nippon (に っ ぽ ん) and Nihon (に ほ ん).
